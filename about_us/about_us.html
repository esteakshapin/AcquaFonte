About Us

At Where's my Water, we believe that we can give people the most convenient alternative to single-use plastic water bottles, which contribute nearly 40% of our plastic waste, equivalent to over 60 million plastic bottles per day. We can provide the means to a world where everybody can contribute to help the community, as well as others outside of the community, into a new world where single-use plastics won't dominate our decisions. We can be a catalyst for people to help sustain our environment for future generations to come. We truly believe that we can change the world, one bottle at a time.
